Custom directives

Angular Form Messages is not designed to be a complete form generator solution such as Angular Formly.

Nevertheless this gives a lot of freedom to have custom validation and custom directives to show validation messages.

Period

The period directive validates two date fields. The From date must be before the To date.

When one of the dates is invalid, the period directives is valid, but the inner date directives invalidate.

Food

The checkboxes have a checkboxes-required directive added that validate if one or more checkboxes are selected.
